Get answers to the most common questions about our HR consulting services and expertise.

What industries do you specialize in for recruitment?
We specialize in IT, Media & Communications, SAP/Oracle/Microsoft, Agriculture, Engineering & Mining, Health and Safety, and Construction Industries. Our consultants receive monthly upskilling to stay current with industry trends and requirements.
Are you CETA accredited for training programs?
Yes, we are fully CETA accredited and offer comprehensive training programs across various industries. Our accreditation ensures that all training meets national standards and provides valuable skills development opportunities.
