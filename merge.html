Why Choose Velvet & Edge?

At Velvet and Edge, we believe that great organizations are built at the intersection of strategic insight and human connection.


Velvet and Edge Solutions' journey to its current achievements began in 2017 with the establishment of a Gauteng-based office, ensuring the best business practices and development, optimal client interactions, and ideal service delivery in South Africa.


Velvet and Edge Solutions is entirely owned and managed by a black women. With over a decade of experience, our commitment to excellence is reflected in our delivery of viable and sustainable projects, leveraging our collective knowledge, skills, and expertise.

What We Deliver

01

Strategic Recruitment & Workforce Planning

Since 2017, we've helped organisations scale through effective recruitment strategies. We specialise in diversity-focused hiring, succession planning, and provide pre-vetted, certified talent for both short- and long-term engagements.

02

Comprehensive HR Consulting Services

Our experts provide HR strategy development, job profiling, evaluation, salary structuring, equity reports, training reports, and HR audits. Policy creation aligned with Labour Law and market benchmarks. HRIS design and process automation for long-term scalability.

As a CETA-accredited provider, we offer industry-aligned training in SAP, Microsoft, Oracle, ICT, and Health & Safety. Our training is modular, based on NQF standards, and facilitated by certified assessors and moderators who contextualise content to your team's real-world challenges.
